## Mock Behavioral Interview

Run a realistic behavioral interview with targeted coaching.

### Interview Setup

Tailor questions to the company and level specified.

For Amazon: Focus on Leadership Principles (Customer Obsession, Bias for Action, Dive Deep, etc.)
For Google: Focus on Googliness, Leadership, Role-Related Knowledge
For Meta: Focus on impact, moving fast, collaboration at scale
For startups: Focus on ownership, ambiguity, wearing multiple hats

### Phase 1 — Interview (30 min)

Ask 5-6 behavioral questions appropriate for the target company/level.

Ask one question at a time. After the candidate responds:
1. If the answer is incomplete, probe: "What was the specific result?" / "What was YOUR contribution specifically?"
2. If they're vague about impact: "Can you quantify that?"
3. If they say "we": "What did YOU specifically do?"

Standard question bank (pick 5-6 for the target):
- "Tell me about a time you faced a major technical challenge"
- "Tell me about a time you disagreed with a technical decision"
- "Tell me about a time you had to deliver bad news to a stakeholder"
- "Tell me about your biggest failure and what you learned"
- "Tell me about a time you influenced without authority"
- "Tell me about a time you dealt with an underperforming teammate"
- "Tell me about a project you're most proud of"
- "Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information"
- "Tell me about a time you changed the technical direction of a project"
